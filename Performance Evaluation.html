(Character Advancement, and "Winning")

This section will go over the mechanics surrounding The Corporate Track and The You Track, two different approaches to advancing your character throughout the game. Within those tracks, I'll discuss how either Eagan or Ricken points will upgrade your characters stats in certain circumstances.

In addition to this, Performance Evaluation will also go over the concept of "Winning" the game, whatever that might be. This will be a more esoteric section, as "winning" will look very different for every game, and be based largely on the components of the story being put forward by the GM, and the decisions made by the players.

The content surrounding "winning" will be from two perspectives - one written as the Lumon Orientation Guide, and another written as an illicit pamphlet once again stuffed into the guide, detailing what it looks like to win against Lumon.
