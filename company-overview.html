Company Overview

Greetings, new hire!

Welcome, <first name, last initial> to your first day at Lumon Industries!

Now that you have completed your initial orientation in our Waking Chamber (correctly answering all five questions), have found your department, and have watched a video recording from your "outie", you have next been handed me: your new hire orientation guide! It will be my great pleasure in the coming minutes (and hopefully hours or days) to steer your learnings and get you up to speed as a productive member of our severed floor.

By now, many of your concerns should already be addressed. But we understand you might still be ruminating on questions such as:

  • Where am I?

  • Who am I?

  • Why am I here?

We want you to feel at peace with the answers we provide you to these questions. So, I will endeavor to readdress them sequentially for you now - giving equal care and attention to each.

Question 1: Where am I?

Behold, you walk on sacred ground! Rejoice in the decision your outie has made for you. Located in one of the various offices around the globe, before you spans the vast hallways and offices of a Lumon Industries severed floor.

That's right, Lumon Industries.

Oh, you've been living under a rock, have you? Haven't heard of Lumon? Just kidding! Your severance procedure in fact ensures that you would have no previous knowledge of this venerable establishment. So, let me fill you in.

Lumon Industries

Founded by Kier Eagan in 1865, Lumon Industries is the sole world leader in biotechnology today. For well over a century, Lumon employees under the wisdom of Kier have been developing products of the future. Our product offerings have saved lives, and brought smiles to millions of faces.

Now, you get to be a part of Kier's continued vision.

Sustained success like Lumon's isn't by accident. It requires a cohesion that no other corporation in history has been able to obtain and maintain.

Our secret? It's our Nine Core Principles, of course! Your outie already knows them.

Please begin studying the Nine Core Principles at your earliest convenience. More information can be found in the Employee Profile section.

The Lumon Nine Core Principles are the building blocks of our company and our people. When all of our employees earnestly strive to live out each principle, we work as one, achieving great things. We also become the best versions of ourselves! The resulting deduction is that there is no greater place to work. No higher calling. More than anywhere else, we do work that matters.

I hope this explanation of where you are is sufficient. Should you have more questions, please refer again to this document, or speak with your department chief.

Question 2: Who am I?

Well, you're <first name, last initial> of course! And what a <first name, last initial> you are. We can't wait for you to learn more about yourself in the coming days and week. But rest assured that there's already one thing we assuredly know about you:

You belong here.

To help you progress on this adventure of self-discovery, Lumon's Human Resources Performance Evaluators (HRPE's) have developed a personality examination of sorts, that you'll complete during your first week at Lumon. We call it our "Employee Profile" document. Since its implementation we have seen significant response clarity rates when the question "Who are you?" is asked to a severed floor employee.

Please give The Lumon Industries Employee Profile your undivided focus over the next few days. It is imperative you complete it before the end of your first week.

Question 3: Why am I here?

You are here, because your outie made the decision to become employed at Lumon Industries, taking a role on one of our severed floors. As a result, your outie underwent the severance procedure, bifurcating access to memories based on location. When you are at work, you remember work, and nothing outside of it. When you are outside of work, you remember all but your time within the Lumon severed floor.

You are here, because your outie made the best choice they will ever make.

You are here, because your outie sought fit to create you and to give you a life within these walls.

You are here, thanks to your outie's wisdom, and thanks to Kier's generosity.

Dear reader,

Do not forget that at the end of the day, what you are being told by Lumon is propaganda. While yes, their products have helped many, they have hurt many others. Lumon's influence has devastated communities, destroyed families, and corrupted hearts.

No company known for the slogan "you must be cut to heal" could truly care about your healing.

That's all for now.

Best regards,

Concerned Citizen
