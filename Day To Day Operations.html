(Playing the Game as a Player)

This section will detail roughly what story beats and events the GM can throw into their story to make every day feel like a productive work day for their employees.

This can include routine events, and unexpected events, visits from management, visits from other department members, malfunctions, discovery of paraphernalia, melon bars, and whatever else. Tables will be included for various events to draw from, should inspiration be needed.
